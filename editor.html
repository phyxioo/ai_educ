Editor's Note

The Human-Centered Revolution

Artificial Intelligence (AI) holds significant promise for transforming education today by addressing key challenges and enhancing teaching methods. However, as UNESCO highlights, the swift advancement of technology often outpaces our regulatory responses. In the Philippines, we stand at a crucial crossroads where "AI for all" must ensure that innovation does not come at the cost of equity or human dignity.

This issue reflects on the state of our classrooms in 2026. While the Department of Education has paved the way for AI integration, we must remain vigilant. The "toll" on young minds isn't just about screen time; it’s about the potential loss of autonomy and the homogenization of learning. We must ensure that AI remains a tool, never the heart of the educational experience.

Author's Insights
