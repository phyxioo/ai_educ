Introduction

Welcome to Toll of AI in Young Minds. In this edition, we dissect the rapid integration of artificial intelligence within the Philippine educational landscape. From the newly established Education Center for AI Research (E-CAIR) to the ethical frameworks proposed by UNESCO, we provide an in-depth look at how technology is being used to optimize learning while safeguarding the rights of 1.05 million learners nationwide.
